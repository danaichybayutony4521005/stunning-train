Behavior Issues

Barking/Meowing

Meet needs first; teach quiet cue or enrichment.

Chewing/Scratching

Provide toys/scratch posts; redirect & reward.

Separation

Gradual alone-time training; puzzle feeders.

Reactivity

Distance-based training; seek a certified trainer.
