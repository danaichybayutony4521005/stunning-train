Training Basics

  • Positive Reinforcement

    Reward desired behavior immediately.

  • Short Sessions

    5–10 minutes, end on a win.

  • Socialization

    Early, controlled exposure to people/places.

  • Consistency

    Same cues and rules from everyone.
